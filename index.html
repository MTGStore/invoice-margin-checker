💰 Invoice Margin Checker

Paste your PDI invoice data and get instant margin analysis

📋 Invoice Analysis

✅ Copy & Paste: From your PDI invoice, select the table data (starting from "Case Name" row) and paste it below. Make sure to include product names, categories, and the margin % column.

📌 How to Use This Tool

Step 1: Go to your PDI invoice page

Step 2: Locate the invoice table that shows all the retailed items

Step 3: Select the entire table starting from the "Case Name" column header down to the last product

Step 4: Copy the selected data (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)

Step 5: Click the "Paste Invoice Data" tab above

Step 6: Paste the data into the text box (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)

Step 7: Click "Check Margins" button

💡 Tip: Make sure your selection includes: Case Name, Category, and the "Invoice Margin %" column. These are essential for the analysis to work.
